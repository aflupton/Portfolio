About Me

I graduated in 2017 with a BA in Liberal Arts from The Evergreen State College in Olympia, where I studied international politics, conflict, war economies, and the social-political nexus of the MENA region.

I came to Epicodus at the urging of two friends who had previous taken the introductory course, and who had then gone on to work in web development. Prior to enrolling in the course, I had absolutely no experience with coding.

When I'm not in class, I fill the few remaining hours of the day exercising at my gym, skiing, whiling away time on Twitter, and reading political theory.

I'm interested in the intersections between tech, art, and politics, and hope to use the skills I'm acquiring at Epicodus to work in those areas.
